About

Hi! My name is Robert Brasso. I am a Graduate student at Towson University working on my Master of Computer Science degree. The area of study that fascinates me the most is Data Science. With the massive amounts of data that is collected through technology, we have the ability to improve and optimize nearly every facet of our existence. The reason I have made this website is to catalog my steps towards becoming a successful data scientist. I will post all of my projects in the Projects section of my website. If you have any ideas for projects you'd like to see, feel free to reach out to me using the information on the Contacts section of this website. If you need anything in data scientist done, don't hestitate to reach out to me. I'm happy to work on projects big or small. If you don't have the financial means to afford data analysis on a specific, we can always work something else out.

If you have any leads on work or would like to enlist me to do a project for you, please send me a private message on Facebook. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website and I hope what you have found here will be of some use to you!
